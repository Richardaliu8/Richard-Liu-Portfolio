    • World Data Visualization

      March 29th, 2024

      Tableau Data Viz

      Our latest Tableau visualization maps global hunger levels, color-coding each country by severity. The map highlights stark disparities. Sub-Saharan Africa and South Asia, shown in darker shades, face high hunger levels due to conflict, climate change, and economic instability. Conversely, North America and Europe, in lighter shades, have lower hunger levels, thanks to political stability, strong economies, and technological access.

      This map underscores the urgent need to address global food inequality and the power of tools like Tableau in understanding and tackling these issues.
